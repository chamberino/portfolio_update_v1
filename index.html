Hello, I’m Michael.

Background

I’m a film composer turned programmer. While working as an assistant to composer David Kitay (Clueless, Ghost World, Look Who's Talking), my responsibilities leaned heavily on the tech side and I began to realize that’s where my talents really shined. After volunteering to design Kitay’s website and having a blast making it I decided to further develop my skills as a web developer.

I now work as the warehouse general manager for a tech startup that aims to revolutionize the way vintage furniture is bought and sold through an intuitive and streamlined backend.

  • HTML
  • CSS

I’d like to work as a UX designer helping clients create intuitive, accessible, and distinguishably singular products.
